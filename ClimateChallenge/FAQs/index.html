Frequently Asked Questions - Climate Challenge

What is the Climate Challenge?

The Climate Challenge is an interactive way to learn about climate change. You complete a series of activities, including a card flip game, an infographic task, and a real-life action, to fill out your checklist. The goal is to reinforce knowledge and spark curiosity about climate-related issues in a fun and dynamic way. Once you complete your checklist, you can submit it and share your pledge.

How do I participate in the Climate Challenge?

To participate, you need to complete three main activities: the Card Flip game, the Infographic challenge, and a Real-Life Action. After completing these, you mark them on your checklist and then submit it. You can find these activities on the Climate Challenge page.

What are the specific activities in the Climate Challenge?

The activities are designed to be engaging and informative:

  • Card Flip: A game to test your knowledge. Click "Start Card Flip" to begin.
  • Infographic: A task to see if you know your way around climate change areas and disciplines. Click "Start Infographic" to test your skills.
  • Real-Life Action: You'll perform a real-world action related to climate awareness or sustainability and then submit it through the "Submit My Action" button.
Hovering over the images on the Climate Challenge page will give you more information before you start each activity.

How does the checklist work?

The checklist, found on the Climate Challenge page, helps you keep track of the activities you've completed: Card Flip, Infographic, and Real-Life Action. As you complete each one, you can tick it off. Once all items are checked, you can use the "Submit Checklist" button.

What happens after I submit my checklist?

After submitting your completed checklist, you are encouraged to share your pledge to the world. This is a way to solidify your commitment to climate action.

Do I need to complete all activities to finish the challenge?

Yes, the aim is to complete all the challenges—Card Flip, Infographic, and Real-Life Action—to fill and submit your checklist.

Where can I find more information or get help if I'm stuck?

You are currently on the FAQ page, which is a great start! For quick info on each activity, remember to tap or hover over the images on the Climate Challenge page. For broader context about the website and its goals, please visit the About Us page.
