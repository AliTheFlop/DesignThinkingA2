Your Personal Pledge

Fact of the day

Recycling one aluminum can saves enough energy to run a TV for 3 hours. Imagine the impact if everyone in Brisbane recycled all their cans!

Everyday Tasks To Help

  • Switch to LED light bulbs.
  • Unplug electronics when not in use.
  • Choose reusable water bottles and coffee cups.
  • Reduce meat consumption by one meal a week.
  • Use public transport, bike, or walk when possible.
  • Support local and sustainable businesses.
