About Us

We are a team of university students who are passionate about addressing the climate crisis through education. This website is part of a proposal for funding from the Brisbane City Council, with the goal of increasing climate change awareness among young people.

Our project aims to inform and empower the next generation by delivering accessible, engaging content tailored specifically for youth. At the heart of our site is the Climate Challenge — an interactive quiz designed to reinforce knowledge and spark curiosity about climate-related issues in a fun and dynamic way.

By combining informative articles with gamified learning, our platform seeks to make climate education more appealing and impactful. With support from the Brisbane City Council, we hope to expand our reach and inspire real-world action from informed, climate-conscious youth.
