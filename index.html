Welcome to Climate Wise

Brisbane City Council is committed to creating a clean, green, and sustainable city. Learn how we are leading the way in climate action and how you can contribute to a low-carbon future.

The Climate Challenge

Join Brisbane's Climate Challenge and make a real difference! Track your daily sustainable actions, earn points for eco-friendly choices, and compete with fellow Brisbanites to reduce our collective carbon footprint. Every small action counts towards our city's goal of becoming a leading sustainable metropolis.

Climate Action Importance

Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges of our time. Brisbane City Council is taking proactive steps to reduce emissions, promote renewable energy, and protect our environment for future generations.

Fun Fact:

Brisbane City Council achieved carbon neutrality in line with the Australian Government's National Carbon Offset Standard in 2017

Achievements

  • Achieved carbon-neutral status in 2017
  • Transitioned to low-carbon city initiatives
  • Implemented sustainable business practices
  • Supported community programs to reduce carbon footprints
  • Promoted renewable energy adoption across the city
