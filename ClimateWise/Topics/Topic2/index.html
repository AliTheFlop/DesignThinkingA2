Rising Sea Levels

Back

Description

Rising sea levels caused by global warming threaten Brisbane’s coastal areas. Flooding can damage homes and natural habitats. Building barriers and protecting mangroves are ways to help keep the coastline safe.

Recent News

Rising sea levels are becoming a growing concern for Brisbane’s coastal suburbs. According to recent reports from the Queensland Government, areas such as Moreton Bay and the Brisbane River delta are already showing signs of increased erosion and flooding. Urban planners are now exploring adaptive infrastructure strategies to help safeguard vulnerable communities.
